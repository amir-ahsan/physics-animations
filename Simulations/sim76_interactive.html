What you are looking at

A moving mass has total energy E = γmc², rest energy mc², and kinetic energy KE = (γ − 1)mc². As speed approaches c, the energy required diverges — which is why no massive object can reach light speed. At low speed the relativistic KE reduces to the familiar ½mv²; near c it blows past it.
