What you are looking at

A conducting rod sliding along rails through a magnetic field generates an emf ε = BLv. The changing area of the circuit changes the magnetic flux, driving a current I = ε/R. That induced current feels a magnetic force opposing the motion — you must keep pushing to keep the rod moving. This is a generator in its simplest form.
