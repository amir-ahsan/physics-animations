What you are looking at

Light bends when it crosses between media of different refractive index, following Snell's law n₁sin θ₁ = n₂sin θ₂. Going into a denser medium (larger n) the ray bends toward the normal; into a rarer medium it bends away. This bending is why a straw looks broken at the water's surface and how lenses focus light.
