What you are looking at

At speeds near light, moving clocks run slow and moving lengths contract. The Lorentz factor γ = 1/√(1 − v²/c²) governs both: a moving clock ticks off Δt = γΔτ in your frame, and a moving ruler shrinks to L = L₀/γ. Below about 10% of c the effect is tiny; approaching c it grows without bound.
