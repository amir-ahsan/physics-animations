What you are looking at

Two parallel plates store charge. Their capacitance C = κε₀A/d grows with plate area and dielectric constant and shrinks as the plates move apart. At a fixed voltage, the field E = V/d and the stored energy U = ½CV². Move the plates closer and the capacitance — and stored energy — climb.
