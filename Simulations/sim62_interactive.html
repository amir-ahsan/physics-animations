What you are looking at

A moving source bunches its sound waves ahead of it and stretches them behind. An observer in front hears a higher frequency, one behind hears lower — the Doppler effect. The observed frequency is f' = f (v ± v_o)/(v ∓ v_s). It's why an ambulance siren drops in pitch the instant it passes you.
