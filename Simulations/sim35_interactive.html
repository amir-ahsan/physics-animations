What you are looking at

A charge fired horizontally between two charged plates feels a constant sideways force F = qE, so it accelerates with a = qE/m and traces a parabola — exactly like projectile motion, but the electric field plays the role of gravity. The horizontal speed is unchanged; only the vertical motion is accelerated. This is how old CRT screens steered their electron beam.
