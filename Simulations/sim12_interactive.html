What you are looking at

A converging lens of focal length f forms an image of an object placed a distance dₒ in front of it. Two rays fix the image: one through the centre (undeviated) and one parallel to the axis (bent through the focal point). The lens equation 1/dₒ + 1/dᵢ = 1/f gives the image distance, and the magnification is m = −dᵢ/dₒ. Move the object and watch the image slide, flip, and change size.
