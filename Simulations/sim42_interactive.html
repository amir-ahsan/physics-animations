What you are looking at

A wire carrying current in a magnetic field feels a force F = ILB sin θ, where θ is the angle between the current and the field. The force is greatest when the wire is perpendicular to the field and vanishes when the wire runs parallel to it. Rotate the wire and watch the force rise and fall like a sine wave.
