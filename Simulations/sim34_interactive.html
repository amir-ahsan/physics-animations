What you are looking at

Two point charges store electric potential energy U = kq₁q₂/r. Opposite charges have negative energy (a well — they are bound, and it takes work to separate them); like charges have positive energy (a hill — they push apart). The force is the negative slope of this curve, F = −dU/dr. Change the charges and watch the energy curve flip between a well and a hill.
