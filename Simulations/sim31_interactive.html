What you are looking at

Two point charges exert equal and opposite forces on each other, F = k|q₁q₂|/r². Like charges repel, opposite charges attract, and the force weakens as the inverse square of the separation — halve the distance and the force quadruples. Change the charges and the gap and watch the force arrows and the F–r curve.
