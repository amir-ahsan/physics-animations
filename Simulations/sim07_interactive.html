What you are looking at

A charge moving through a uniform magnetic field feels a force F = qvB that is always perpendicular to its velocity. That force never speeds the particle up — it only bends its path — so the motion is a circle of radius r = mv/(qB). Change the mass, charge, speed, or field and watch the circle grow or shrink; flip the charge's sign and watch the curve reverse.
