What you are looking at

Current is a river of charge carriers drifting slowly through a wire: I = n q A v_d. Even a large current means a tiny drift speed, because there are enormous numbers of carriers (large n). A wider wire or denser carrier gas carries the same current at an even slower drift. Watch the carriers crawl while the current stays healthy.
