What you are looking at

Every particle has a wavelength λ = h/p = h/(mv). Light, slow particles have long wavelengths and show wave behavior; heavy, fast ones have wavelengths so tiny they act purely as particles. This is why electrons diffract but baseballs don't. Change the momentum and watch the wave stretch or shrink.
