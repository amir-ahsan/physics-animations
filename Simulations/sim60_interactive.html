What you are looking at

When a hot object meets a cold one in an insulated container, heat flows until they reach a common final temperature. Energy is conserved: the heat lost by the hot body equals the heat gained by the cold one, m₁c₁(T_f − T₁) + m₂c₂(T_f − T₂) = 0. Solving gives the equilibrium temperature — a mass- and specific-heat-weighted average.
