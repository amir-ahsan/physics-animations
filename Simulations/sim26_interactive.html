What you are looking at

Torque is turning effectiveness: τ = rF sinφ, where r is the distance from the pivot to where the force is applied and φ is the angle between them. Equivalently, τ = Fℓ with the lever arm ℓ = r sinφ — the perpendicular distance from the pivot to the line of the force. A force along the wrench (φ = 0) does nothing; perpendicular (φ = 90°) does the most.
