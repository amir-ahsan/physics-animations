What you are looking at

A traveling wave y = A sin(2π(x/λ − ft)) moves steadily at speed v = fλ. A standing wave is what you get when two such waves travel in opposite directions on a string fixed at both ends: the pattern stays put, with nodes that never move and antinodes that oscillate. Only certain harmonics n fit. Press Play and compare how the crests of one travel while the nodes of the other stay fixed.
