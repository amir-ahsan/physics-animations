What you are looking at

A particle moves at constant speed v around a circle of radius R. Its velocity always points along the tangent, but its direction is constantly turning, so it has a centripetal acceleration ac = v²/R pointing to the centre, supplied by a force Fc = mv²/R. Speed is constant; velocity is not. Press Play and watch the two vectors.
