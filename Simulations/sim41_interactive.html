What you are looking at

A charged particle entering a uniform magnetic field curves in a circle of radius r = mv/(qB). Heavier particles swing wider, stronger fields tighten the arc. The detector position is 2r from the entry, so measuring where each ion lands reveals its mass — the heart of every mass spectrometer.
