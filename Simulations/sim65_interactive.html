What you are looking at

Light reflecting off the top and bottom of a thin film interferes with itself — the colors in soap bubbles and oil slicks. The extra path is 2nt through the film, plus a half-wave flip if the reflection is off a denser medium. Constructive reflection needs 2nt = (m + ½)λ (with one phase flip). Change the thickness and watch which wavelengths brighten.
