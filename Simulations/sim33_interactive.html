What you are looking at

Field lines are curves that follow the electric field. They start on positive charges and end on negative, never cross, and crowd together where the field is strong. They are always perpendicular to the equipotential curves. Change the two charges to morph between a dipole (opposite) and a like-charge pattern.

Field lines

lines run from + to −
denser lines = stronger field
Blue curves = field lines; they leave positive charges and enter negative ones.

Equipotential curves

Curves of constant potential (green = positive, blue = negative). Field lines always cross them at right angles.

What each plot shows
