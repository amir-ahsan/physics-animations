What you are looking at

A cart slides on a frictionless valley track. As it drops, potential energy U = mgy converts to kinetic energy K = ½mv², but the total stays constant: K + U = mgy₀. Its speed at any height is v = √(2g(y₀ − y)) — independent of the path or the mass. Watch the energy trade back and forth as the cart swings.

Energy bars
