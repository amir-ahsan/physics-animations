What you are looking at

Crossed electric and magnetic fields let only one speed pass straight through. The electric force qE pushes one way, the magnetic force qvB the other. They cancel exactly when v = E/B — that is the selected speed. Faster particles bend one way, slower ones the other; only the matched speed goes undeflected.

Net force vs. speed

Net = qE − qvB. It crosses zero exactly at v = E/B — the selected speed.
