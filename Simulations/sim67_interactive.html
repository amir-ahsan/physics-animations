What you are looking at

When light tries to leave a denser medium for a rarer one, past a certain incidence angle it can't escape at all — it reflects entirely back inside. That threshold is the critical angle θ_c = arcsin(n₂/n₁). Below it, light refracts out; at it, the refracted ray grazes the surface; beyond it, total internal reflection. This is the principle behind fiber optics.
