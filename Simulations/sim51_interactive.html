What you are looking at

An ideal gas obeys PV = nRT. Squeeze the volume and the pressure rises; heat the gas and it pushes harder or expands. The molecules bounce around inside, and their collisions with the walls are the pressure. Change any of P, V, n, or T and the others adjust to keep the equation balanced — here we hold n fixed and read off P from V and T.
