What you are looking at

Entropy counts the number of microscopic arrangements (microstates) that look the same macroscopically: S = k ln Ω. For N gas molecules free to be on either side of a box, the number of ways to get a given split is a binomial count. The most probable split is 50–50 because it has by far the most microstates — which is why gases spontaneously spread out and never un-mix.
