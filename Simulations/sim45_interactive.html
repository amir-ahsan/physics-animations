What you are looking at

A tightly wound coil produces a nearly uniform field inside, B = μ₀nI, where n is turns per unit length. Pack the turns closer or push more current and the interior field strengthens; outside an ideal solenoid the field is essentially zero. This is how electromagnets and MRI magnets are built.
