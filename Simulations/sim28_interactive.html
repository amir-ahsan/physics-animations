What you are looking at

A wheel of radius R rolls without slipping at angular speed ω. The rolling condition ties rotation to translation: vcenter = ωR. The contact point is momentarily at rest, the center moves at ωR, and the top moves at 2ωR. A point on the rim traces a graceful cycloid. Press Play and watch the rim point rise and fall.
