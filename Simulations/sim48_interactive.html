What you are looking at

An inductor resists sudden changes in current. Close the switch and the current climbs gradually toward its final value V/R, following I(t) = (V/R)(1 − e−t/τ) with time constant τ = L/R. A larger inductance or smaller resistance means a slower rise. The inductor "smooths" the turn-on.
