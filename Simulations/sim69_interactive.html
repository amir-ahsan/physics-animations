What you are looking at

Polarized light passing through a second polarizer (the "analyzer") is dimmed according to Malus's law I = I₀cos²θ, where θ is the angle between their axes. Aligned axes pass everything; crossed axes (90°) block all the light. Rotate the analyzer and watch the transmitted brightness follow a cos² curve.
