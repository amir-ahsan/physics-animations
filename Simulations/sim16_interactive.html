What you are looking at

A boat aims across a river at heading angle θ with speed vᵇ relative to the water, while the current carries the water downstream at vᶜ. The boat's velocity relative to the ground is the vector sum v⃗bg = v⃗bw + v⃗wg. That sum decides the real path, the crossing time, and how far downstream it drifts. Adjust the heading and current and watch the boat's true track.
