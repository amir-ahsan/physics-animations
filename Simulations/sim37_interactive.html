What you are looking at

Three resistors can be wired end-to-end (series, resistances add: R = R₁+R₂+R₃) or side-by-side (parallel, reciprocals add: 1/R = 1/R₁+1/R₂+1/R₃). Series always gives a larger equivalent than any single resistor; parallel always gives a smaller one. The battery voltage then sets the total current I = V/R.

The circuit

R_eq = – Ω
total current I = V/R = – A
power P = I²R = – W
