What you are looking at

Air columns resonate at specific frequencies set by their length and end conditions. An open–open pipe supports all harmonics f_n = n·v/2L; a closed–open pipe supports only odd harmonics f_n = n·v/4L (n = 1, 3, 5…). Open ends are displacement antinodes, closed ends are nodes. Pick the pipe type and harmonic to see the standing-wave pattern and its frequency.
