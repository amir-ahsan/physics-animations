What you are looking at

Two carts collide on a frictionless track. Momentum is always conserved: m₁v₁ + m₂v₂ = m₁v₁′ + m₂v₂′. In an elastic collision kinetic energy is also conserved; in a perfectly inelastic one the carts stick and move together, losing the most kinetic energy possible. Set the masses and speeds, choose the type, and press Play.
