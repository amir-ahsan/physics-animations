What you are looking at

Two coherent sources send out waves that overlap and interfere. Where the path difference is a whole number of wavelengths the waves add (bright fringe, d sin θ = mλ); where it's a half-integer they cancel (dark). On a distant screen this makes evenly spaced fringes with spacing Δy ≈ λL/d. Closer slits or longer wavelength spread the fringes apart.
