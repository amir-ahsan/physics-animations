What you are looking at

Two tones of nearly equal frequency add up to a single tone that slowly swells and fades — beats. The combined wave oscillates at the average frequency inside an envelope that pulses at the beat frequency f_beat = |f₁ − f₂|. Musicians tune by ear this way: when the beating slows to a stop, the two notes match.
